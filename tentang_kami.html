About

Not only is it known for its rich culture and natural beauty, Indonesia also has a variety of culinary delights that can attract the hearts of tourists to stop at Mother Earth. Yes, the culinary archipelago is now increasingly showing its charm in the eyes of the world.
Indonesian specialties began to be widely known by many people from all over the world. Unmitigated, some of them even got into the list of the most delicious foods in the world, such as Rendang.
Not only rendang, culinary archipelago which is very diverse is actually a unique and distinctive characteristics. Especially considering that every ethnic group and region has its own unique culinary, like a dessert.
On this website we hope to be able to promote and develop Indonesia's cultural heritage in the traditional culinary scene. where our culinary which used to be rarely seen, even by the Indonesian people themselves now we hope to be able to be viewed and accepted by the international community.
