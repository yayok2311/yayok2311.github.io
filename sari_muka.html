Sari Muka Cake

There is one cake that has a unique name that is face sari cake. This ssari advance cake is one of the cakes originating from South Kalimantan. Cakes made by steaming are made from glutinous rice or rice flour, brown sugar, granulated sugar and other ingredients which are then steamed until cooked. The taste of this sweet and delicious face cake can be easily found in a traditional market in Banjarmasin.
