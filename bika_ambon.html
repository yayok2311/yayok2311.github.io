Ambon Bika Cake

Bika ambon is a kind of confectionary from Indonesia. Made from ingredients such as eggs, sugar and coconut milk, bika ambon is generally sold with pandan flavor, although now other flavors are also available such as durian, cheese, and chocolate. Bika ambon usually can survive in the best condition for about four days because after that the cake starts to harden.
According to the explanation of M Muhar Omtatok, a historian and historian, ambon bika cake is inspired by the typical Malay cake, Bika or Bingka. Furthermore, it was modified with developer material in the form of palm sap / palm wine until hollow and different from the typical Malay Bika or Bingka cake. Furthermore, M. Muhar Omtatok mentioned that this cake was called bika ambon because it was first sold and popular at the intersection of Jl Ambon-Sei Kera Medan.
Bika Ambon is known as a souvenir of the city of Medan, North Sumatra. In Medan, Jalan Mojopahit in the Medan Petisah area there are at least 30 shops selling this cake. [1] Each store in this location can sell more than 1,000 packs of bika ambon per day if it is approaching the feast. [1] It is estimated, the name bika ambon arises from the habits of the people who were new to bika produced in Ambon, Medan. The mention of bika ambon has finally become a tradition along with the development of this food industry.
